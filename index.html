Why PyClef exists

A practical bridge between OMR research and usable musical output.

Most object detectors can find musical symbols, but raw bounding boxes are not enough to reconstruct a score. PyClef adds explicit staff modeling, clef-aware pitch reconstruction, rhythm grouping and export routines so detections become playable and inspectable results.

Neural detection

YOLOv11s is used to detect dense musical notation classes from DeepScoresV2-style inputs.

MIRP refinement

The MIRP layer constrains detections with staff geometry, clefs, braces and score structure.

Pipeline

From image to music-ready artifacts.

01

Detect

Read PDF or image pages and locate staves, braces, clefs, notes, rests and accidentals.

02

Refine

Apply MIRP rules to organize detections into musical context instead of isolated boxes.

03

Reconstruct

Map vertical positions to pitch, align systems and prepare playback events.

04

Export

Generate annotated score pages, MIDI, MP3 and a synchronized video preview.
