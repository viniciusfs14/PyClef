Evaluation

Detection quality and reconstruction quality are reported separately.

PyClef separates object-detection metrics from functional pitch reconstruction metrics, since high mAP does not necessarily imply correct musical reconstruction.

mAP

YOLOv11S reached 0.4126 mAP@[0.5:0.95] on DeepScoresV2 Dense.

TRA

With YOLOv11S, MIRP increased TRA from 0.04 to 0.975.

Model trade-off

MIRP reduces the practical gap between model sizes.

YOLOv11L reached 0.51379 mAP, but both YOLOv11S and YOLOv11L exceeded 97% TRA after MIRP.
