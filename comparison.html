Evaluation

Detection metrics are only the first half of OMR.

PyClef reports detector performance, but emphasizes functional reconstruction: whether the symbols become correct musical pitch and usable playback artifacts.

mAP

The YOLOv11S detector reached 0.4126 mAP@[0.5:0.95] on DeepScoresV2 Dense.

TRA

MIRP increased pitch reconstruction from 4% to 97.5% with YOLOv11S.

Model trade-off

The selected model balances detection quality and runtime cost.

Larger YOLO variants can improve raw detection metrics, but PyClef prioritizes a practical pipeline where structural refinement has a major effect on the final musical result.
